Hello.. I'm Luke and welcome! As a professional product illustrator, I sit within the marketing team of any sports or active wear designer or manufacturer companies I partner with. I've had a lifetime affinity for the fine arts, with a focus on canvas painting, which I studied in school, college, and university. Due to my passion for the sport, my attention evolved over time to digital illustrations, notably in the context of basketball shoes, as this is a sport I hold dear to my heart. My distinctive style is characterised by vivid colours, minute details, and dramatic compositions. I produce eye-catching illustrations for print advertisements, social media campaigns, and other promotional items. I constantly push the limits of digital illustration by fusing technical know-how with proficiency in art and design. My work not only looks fantastic, but it also boosts brand recognition and increases sales.. I appreciate you learning more about who I am and what I do.
