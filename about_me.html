Hello!

Im Luke and welcome!

As an illustrator embedded within the marketing team of a sports/active shoe companies, my role is to bring a unique blend of creativity and artistic expertise to the table. I've always had a passion for art since I was young, which eventually led me to study it at school and university. However, over time, my interest in art evolved into a fascination with digital illustrations, specifically in the context of basketball shoes, given my love for the sports.

My style is known for its vibrant colours, intricate details, and dynamic compositions, all of which help to make the advertising campaigns I work on stand out from the crowd. Whether it's creating illustrations for print ads, social media campaigns, or other advertising materials, I focus on producing work that is colourful and visually stunning.

I am constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible with digital illustration. By combining my technical skills with my passion for art and design, I am able to create work that not only looks great but also helps to drive sales and grow brand presence.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my work, and I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you!
